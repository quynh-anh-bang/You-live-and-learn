Giới thiệu về blog

you live and learn: said when you hear or discover something that is surprising (Cambridge Dictionary)

This blog is an attempt to compile what makes me, a high school senior majoring in biology, go "Oh well, you live and learn". To be honest, that's basically my life in a nutshell.

One of the biggest theme of this blog is science-backed self-help, that is advice based on research in related fields such as neuroscience, positive psychology and so on.
